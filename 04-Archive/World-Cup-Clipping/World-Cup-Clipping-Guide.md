## Summary
Beginner guide to earning from the 2026 FIFA World Cup (June 11 – July 19) by creating short vertical clips for TikTok, Facebook Reels, and similar platforms.

## Key Points
- Start dedicated football accounts (don't mix niches)
- Free tools: CapCut for editing, Opus Clip for AI-assisted clipping
- Sign up on clipping marketplaces: Whop and Reach (pay-per-view campaigns)
- Source: public FIFA footage + add original commentary, overlays, or reactions
- Post consistently daily, timed to matches
- Hook style: emotional moments, controversies, drama — not just goals
- Income layers: view-based payouts + platform monetization + brand deals + account sales

## Copyright Reality Check
Raw highlights = flagged. Adding commentary, text overlay, or analysis = generally safe. The value-add is both the legal shield and the differentiation.

## Notable Comment
"It depends on how you use the footage. If you just repost raw match highlights with nothing added, yes you'll get flagged. But if you add your own commentary, reaction, text overlay or analysis on top, you're generally safe."
