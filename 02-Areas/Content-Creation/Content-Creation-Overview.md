## Tools Mentioned
- CapCut (free editing)
- Opus Clip (AI clipping)
- Whop + Reach (clipping marketplaces)
- FIFA public footage with original commentary/overlays added (avoids copyright flags)

## Key Rule on Copyright
Raw repost = flagged. Add commentary, text overlay, or analysis = generally safe.

- Platform focus: TikTok & YouTube Shorts first due to reach.
