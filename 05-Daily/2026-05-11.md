## Key Learnings / Insights
*Agent*
- **Infrastructure Integrity**: We learned that in environments with power instability, **Git reflogs** are your best friend. We successfully recovered from a total repo "crash" by manually tracing back to the last healthy commit and re-indexing the files.
- **The "Premium Download" Pattern**: We discovered that raw Supabase URLs aren't enough for a professional marketplace. By building a **Proxy API**, we gained control over the user's file manager, allowing us to enforce **human-readable filenames** and **forced downloads**, which significantly elevates the brand feel.
- **Strategic Sequencing**: We validated the insight that **product journey > marketing foundation**. Finishing the secure Library flow (Phase 4) was the right move before diving into deep SEO, as it ensures that traffic we eventually attract actually results in a successful "moment of value."
- **Persistence is Professionalism**: Transitioning the Wishlist from local-only to a **Supabase-backed system** makes Tempire feel like a real application rather than a "toy" marketplace. Using **Optimistic UI** for this ensures the app still feels fast despite the database sync.
